LSG - Lycée Secondaire Gremda

the high school of gremda or LSG located in Sfax,Tunisia . it is a public school and it's been founded since the year 2009 , it served a lot of students and also provided so many working jobs in the schools. It has been very successful in many domains including science and technology

nowadays LSG has the highest baccalaureate pass rates with 95% of students obtaining a bachelor's degree, while it has the lowest failure rate among all other subjects. LSG has a good reputation in the Sfax region. Its main purpose is to educate the youth about the importance of education and how it can be beneficial for the future development of society. It is an important part of society that helps in developing their self-esteem and self-confidence.
